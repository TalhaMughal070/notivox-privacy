Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Protected

Notivox is a fully offline notes app. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data — ever.

No Data Collected No Internet Required No Account Needed No Ads No Tracking

1. App Overview

App Name: Notivox

Package: com.notivox.app

Type: Productivity — Notes App

Notivox is a local-only note-taking app that allows users to create, edit, search, and delete notes. All data is stored exclusively on the user's device using local storage. The app does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any external server, API, or cloud service.

We confirm that no data is collected or shared with third parties. All notes are stored locally on your device using Hive (a local database). This data never leaves your device.

3. Local Data Storage

📱

On-Device Only

All notes (title, content, timestamps) are stored using Hive local database on your device's internal storage. No cloud backup, no remote sync.

🔒

No Transmission

Your notes are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any server, API, third-party service, or other device.

🗑️

Easy Deletion

You can delete individual notes at any time within the app. Uninstalling Notivox permanently removes all locally stored data from your device.

5. Third-Party Services

Notivox does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or social media platforms.

There are no Firebase, Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, Crashlytics, AdMob, or any similar integrations in this app.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Notivox does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for all ages as it stores data locally only and has no network communication.

This app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy.

7. Data Security

🛡️

No Network = No Risk

Because no data ever leaves your device, there is no risk of data breach, unauthorized server access, or data interception in transit.

📦

Local Database (Hive)

Notes are stored using the Hive NoSQL database which operates entirely within the app's sandboxed local storage on Android.
